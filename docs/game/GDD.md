# Ghost x Zoro Grand Line TCG Game Design Document

## Core Fantasy

Ghost and Zoro board a private One Piece strategy hub where a trading-card collection becomes a playable Grand Line map. The game is a playable research dashboard: move, talk, inspect, compare, decide.

## Player Loop

1. Pass the private password gate.
2. Choose Luffy or Zoro.
3. Spawn on the Thousand Sunny / island trading post hub.
4. Walk to NPCs and landmarks.
5. Unlock strategy notes by inspecting cards, bid rows, source ledgers, and media archive entries.
6. Add or import new cards, update the local portfolio, and export JSON.

## Controls

- WASD or arrows: move.
- Mouse: camera.
- E: interact.
- Esc: menu/back.

## NPC Roles

- Nami: portfolio value, concentration, and sell/hold warnings.
- Robin: source-backed research, release calendar, and PDF dossier archive.
- Franky: card asset vault, shipments, slabs, media previews.
- Usopp: open bids, cancel/keep recommendations, duplicate warnings.
- Sanji: buying/selling routes, Cape Town/local store/event plan.
- Chopper: risk controls, low-grade traps, grading-company warnings.
- Brook: video/PDF theater and hidden soundtrack surprise.

## Main Areas

- Spawn Deck: title and character identity.
- Card Vault: owned card pedestals and slab inspections.
- Bounty Board: 13 open Courtyard offers with exposure and action status.
- Robin Archive: research source ledger, report/PDF links, release calendar.
- Sanji Market: Courtyard, Phygitals, TCGplayer, Cardmarket, eBay/PSA Vault, local Cape Town channels.
- Chopper Clinic: risk checklist and low-grade warning table.
- Brook Theater: prior dossier/report/video/audio archive.

## Acceptance Slice

The first playable slice must include a real data-backed hub, character select, movement, at least six NPC interactions, owned-card pedestals, open-bid board, watchlist/release notes, manual card entry, JSON import/export, and archive links.
