# Research Notes

Private-use research captured on 2026-05-24 for the Ghost x Zoro One Piece TCG investment video.

## Core Market Read

PSA reported a broad grading-demand boom, not a One Piece-only boom: PSA says it graded 2 million cards in 2020, more than 19 million in 2025, and January-April 2026 output grew by nearly 2.3 million items, a 39% year-over-year jump. PSA is responding with a USD 200 million infrastructure investment, expanded capacity, and changed turnaround estimates. Source: [PSA-2026-INFRA](https://www.psacard.com/articles/articleview/15715/200-million-investment-grading-experience).

PSA and Bandai are explicitly using One Piece promos as a collector-acquisition surface. PSA says its earlier Straw Hat promo became the most-graded English-language One Piece card at PSA, then announced another Shanks promo for Collectors Club members. Source: [PSA-BANDAI-PROMO](https://www.psacard.com/articles/articleview/15211/ahoy-psa-x-bandai-partner-on-third-exclusive-one-piece-promo).

Bandai Namco's 2025 card-business feature is the strongest official market-growth anchor. It states that the Japanese TCG market reached JPY 319.7 billion in FY2025.3, up 11.7% year over year, that the global market is expected to keep expanding with Japan and North America as key centers, and that Bandai ranked third in global TCG market share as of March 2025. It also says that growth was driven by the One Piece Card Game, released in July 2022. Source: [BANDAI-IR-2025-CARDS](https://www.bandainamco.co.jp/en/ir/library/feature02_01_2025.html).

Do not claim One Piece has recently passed Pokemon in grading volume unless a later source proves it directly. The current verified support is narrower: One Piece demand is rising inside a broader grading and TCG boom, and Bandai/PSA both treat it as strategically important.

## One Piece TCG Context

PSA's basics guide frames One Piece as a long-running manga/anime franchise with modern Bandai TCG success beginning in 2022. The guide also explains why set codes, rarity marks, starter decks, specialty sets, and promos matter to collectors. Source: [PSA-ONEPIECE-BASICS](https://www.psacard.com/info/tcg/one-piece-collecting-basics-guide).

Official 2026 product context matters for watchlists. OP-13, "Carrying on His Will," is positioned as a third-anniversary booster with Luffy, Ace, Sabo, Roger, Imu, special WANTED cards, Super Alt-Art, and Red Super Alt-Art treatments. Source: [OPCG-OP13](https://en.onepiece-cardgame.com/products/boosters/op13/). EB-03, "One Piece Heroines Edition," released February 20, 2026 with USD 4.99 MSRP and a 71+4 type product structure. Source: [OPCG-EB03](https://en.onepiece-cardgame.com/products/boosters/eb03.php).

## Buy/Sell Channels

Cape Town and South Africa: Collect-a-Con Cape Town is the strongest immediate local event signal. Search and site surfaces show June 6, 2026 in Cape Town at Fire and Ice Hotel, with trading cards, vendors, auctions, and collection-selling inquiry. Source: [COLLECTACON-CPT-2026](https://www.collectacon.co.za/). The Card Cache is a Cape Town-based online store covering Pokemon, Magic, One Piece, sealed, singles, and graded cards, and it exposes a "Sell Your Cards" path. Source: [CARD-CACHE-CPT](https://cardcache.co.za/pages/about). TCGXchange is Cape Town-based and useful for broader TCG pricing or sealed-product benchmarking, but its current public surface emphasizes other TCGs more than One Piece. Source: [TCGXCHANGE-CPT](https://tcgxchange.co.za/). Wizards Books and Games should be treated as a local verification target for stock and events before spending.

Global/online: TCGplayer is strongest for North American raw/singles liquidity and seller tooling. Source: [TCGPLAYER-SELLERS](https://seller.tcgplayer.com/). Cardmarket is useful for European comps and demand signals, especially for Japanese/English price gaps. Source: [CARDMARKET-ONEPIECE](https://www.cardmarket.com/en/OnePiece). eBay and PSA Vault remain relevant for slabs, but fees, vault logistics, and cross-border friction must be checked before moving inventory. Source: [EBAY-VAULT-FEES](https://pages.ebay.com/vault/fees/).

## Investment Framing

This is educational scenario analysis, not financial advice. The thesis is not "buy everything One Piece." The thesis is to build a disciplined card book around iconic characters, event/pre-release stamps, low-pop or hard-to-replace promos, English exclusives when liquidity is strongest, and sealed product bought at or near MSRP.
