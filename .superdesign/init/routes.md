# Route Inventory

Empty-repo initialization. This project is a video workspace, not a multi-route web app.

Planned local entry points:

- Remotion composition: `GhostZoroVideo`
- HyperFrames storyboard/check page: `hyperframes/ghost-zoro/index.html`
- Local docs: `docs/research.md`, `docs/strategy-whitepaper.md`, `docs/script.md`, `docs/storyboard.md`, `docs/qa.md`

The Vault Room member-beta app entry points:

- `/` public-safe landing and beta gate.
- `/app` playable 3D Cape Town card-harbor hub.
- `/collection` member collection and import/manual entry.
- `/cards/[id]` card detail, owned status, source notes, price snapshots.
- `/trades` trade board and offer workflow, no payments or escrow.
- `/market` watchlist, price trends, and interest listings.
- `/events` game nights, releases, RSVPs, and calendar.
- `/rankings` player points and collection leaderboard.
- `/group-buys` case interest and bulk grading coordination.
- `/news` OP release/news/lore archive.
- `/admin` moderation queue and beta admin shell.
