# The Vault Room Vault Gacha Demo

Updated: 2026-06-20

## Purpose

Create a public-safe, highly visual `/gacha` page that demonstrates the future Vault Room digital pack-rip product without taking payment or issuing real random rewards yet.

## Reference Patterns

- Phygitals East Blue Pack: clear pack price, EV cue, 100% pull language, recent hits, top hits, redeemable physical winnings.
- CollectorCrypt / Gacha style: large central machine, top-pull board, countdown/social energy, visible prize board.
- Courtyard vending pattern: pack/category browsing, trading-card product photography, digital-first reveal with physical redemption path.

## Experience Contract

- First screen must feel like a premium Vault Room arcade/vault machine, not a plain shop page.
- The machine is a real interactive React Three Fiber scene, supported by the generated hero art layer.
- Users can choose a pack tier, trigger a demo rip, see the machine animate, reveal a real catalogue-backed item, and preview either redeem/ship or 70% FMV auto-sell.
- Payment labels are visible but disabled: Crypto, Visa, Mastercard, EFT, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, Stripe, PayFast.
- Copy must clearly say this is a visual demo / coming soon until payment, legal rules, published odds, reserve controls, refund terms, and shipping terms are finalized.

## Public Safety

- Do not process payments, reserve inventory, or imply the random reward product is live.
- Do not show internal owner/source notes, staff floors, cost basis, or consignment owner labels.
- Prize candidates come from public catalogue products only.
- The 70% FMV sell-back is a simulated preview, not a binding offer until live terms exist.

## Visual Direction

- Parchment sea-map background, navy/gold/coral Vault Room palette, glossy high-segment 3D gacha/vault machine, glass dome, capsule glow, card/slab reveal, top-hit board.
- Mobile stacks into hero art, 3D machine, pack controls, reveal panel, and prize tiles without text overlap.
- Motion must respect reduced-motion preferences.
